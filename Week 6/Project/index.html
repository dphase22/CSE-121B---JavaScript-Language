What is Digimons

Digimon is a Japanese media franchise that includes anime, manga, video games, and other products related to digital monsters or "Digimon". The franchise was created in 1997 by Bandai, Toei Animation, and WiZ and has been popular among children and anime fans worldwide ever since.

The storyline of Digimon revolves around children who enter a digital world filled with these creatures. They eventually form a bond with their own personal Digimon, who evolves and grows stronger as they progress through the digital world. The franchise has had several iterations, each with its own unique story and set of characters. Throughout the franchise, the themes of friendship, trust, and courage are emphasized, as the children and their Digimon work together to save the digital world and the real world from destruction.
